The Administration application enables account administrators to manage their users, roles, tenants, applications and business rules and lets them configure a number of settings for their account. 

The following sections will walk you through all functionalities of the Administration application in detail. 

>**Important**: For information on the user and permission management in the Administration application, refer to the separate section on [User and Permission Management](/guides/images/users-guide/user-and-permissions-management).
