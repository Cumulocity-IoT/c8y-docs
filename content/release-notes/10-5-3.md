### Improvement

[**Administration**] Usability in the **Logs** tab of microservices has been further improved. The date specifying the time range can now be selected from a calendar and the theme can be selected through a toggle. For details see [Administration > Managing applications](/guides/user-guide/administration#managing-applications) in the User guide.[MTM-26441]
