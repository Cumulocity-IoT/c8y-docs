This release of the Cumulocity IoT OEE app addresses several important calculation issues as well as various smaller UI improvements & enhancements for icons, dashboards, charts & the profile editor.

The most important change to be highlighted is the storing of OEE values in just 1 measurement with 20 fragments instead of 20 measurements. With this change, the number of measurements per profile and calculation interval has been significantly reduced.
